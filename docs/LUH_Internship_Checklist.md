# LUH Mechanical Engineering Internship Administrative Checklist

This document clarifies the administrative requirements for the "Fachpraktikum" at Leibniz University Hannover (Faculty of Mechanical Engineering).

## 🚨 Crucial Answer: Do I need to submit documents *before* starting?

**Generally, NO.**
According to the official FAQ of the Praktikantenamt:
> "Muss ich mein Praktikum vor Antritt beim Praktikantenamt anmelden?"
> **"Nein, eine Anmeldung vor Antritt ist nicht erforderlich."** (Standard rule for most German universities, but verify specifically for your exam regulations).

**However, there are exceptions and recommended steps:**

1.  **Mandatory Internship Certificate (Pflichtpraktikumsbescheinigung):**
    *   Most companies (like NIFE/MHH) require a certificate from the university stating that the internship is mandatory.
    *   **Action:** You need to apply for this *before* signing the contract if NIFE asks for it.
    *   **Where:** Request via email to `praktikum@maschinenbau.uni-hannover.de`.

2.  **Supervisor Approval (Mentorenprüfung):**
    *   While not a formal "registration," it is **highly recommended** to check your planned tasks with a professor (Mentor/Prüfer) at LUH to ensure they will be recognized later.
    *   **Why:** To avoid the risk of doing 12 weeks of work that the Praktikantenamt later rejects as "not engineering-related enough."

## ✅ Checklist: Before, During, After

### Phase 1: Before Internship (Now)
- [ ] **Check Exam Regulations (Prüfungsordnung):** Confirm that a 12-week Fachpraktikum is required for your specific Master's degree.
- [ ] **Get "Pflichtpraktikumsbescheinigung":** If NIFE HR requires it for the contract.
- [ ] **Sign Contract:** Ensure it covers the full 12 weeks.
- [ ] **(Optional but Recommended) Mentor Check:** Briefly show your project plan (from `docs/NIFE_Internship_Plan.md`) to your IKM supervisor to confirm it counts as "Fachpraktikum".

### Phase 2: During Internship
- [ ] **Daily Log:** Keep a rough bullet-point log of what you did each day/week. This makes writing the final report much easier.
- [ ] **NDA Check:** Clarify with NIFE supervisors what data can be published in your university report.

### Phase 3: After Internship (Recognition)
- [ ] **Write Report:** 20-30 pages, technical focus.
- [ ] **Get Certificate (Zeugnis):** Official document from NIFE.
- [ ] **Submit to Praktikantenamt:**
    - Digital submission via email (PDF).
    - Application form (Antrag auf Anerkennung).
    - Report + Certificate.
